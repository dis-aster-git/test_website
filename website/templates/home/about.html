ABOUT US

Our Mission:

Zero victims for masonry residents.

How do we solve:

We solve this problem by bringing together the best experts' wisdom from all fields of industry, government, and academia across the boundaries of organizations and borders.

What is Masonry?

Masonry structures, which are simply built up of stones and bricks, are used all over the world. Since bricks and stones have extremely high weather resistance and durability as building materials, buildings with a building age of several hundred years usually exist. In addition, masonry is cool in summer, warm in winter, does not cause fire, does not require advanced technology for construction, bricks and stones as raw materials can be obtained anywhere, and it has many advantages such as cheap price. It has become popular and continues to be built today. Today, 60% of the world's population lives in masonry dwellings.

MASONRY CHALLENGES

Masonry, which is home to 60% of the world's population, is extremely vulnerable to earthquakes, and about 80% of earthquake victims over the last 100 years have been caused by earthquakes. Since masonry is widely distributed in the world and coincides with earthquake-prone areas, we have been causing earthquake victims every year since ancient times. In masonry, because each brick and stone behave individually, it was not possible to accurately simulate the process of a building receiving a seismic wave and breaking from where it cracked until it collapsed. Seismic diagnosis could not be done either. We are the world's first company that can develop technology from structural analysis of masonry to implementation of seismic reinforcement and carry out it all at once.
