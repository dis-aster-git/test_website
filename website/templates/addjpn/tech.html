OUR TECHNOLOGY

TECHNOLOGICAL SOLUTION:

Two techniques are needed to seismically reinforce masonry. One is seismic design, and the other is reinforcement technology (reinforcement method). (1) For seismic design, structural calculation is performed to evaluate the seismic performance of the current building, and the lacking seismic performance is clarified. Next, the reinforcement design is performed considering what part of the building and how much reinforcement is required. ② Reinforcement technology basically only coats the outer wall with high strength resin. Tensile strength and properties can be adjusted by the fiber content and coating thickness. It is environmentally friendly as it uses acrylic silicon resin as the base material. Since we have the basic technology to make slurry by dispersing and mixing various fibers and resins, we can develop and manufacture various products according to the application and needs.

Power coating manufacturing requires advanced technology, but it is very easy to use.
